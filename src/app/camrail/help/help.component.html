How To make a bill?

Click on the new Invoice button and just fill the inputs corresponding to each article.
NB : press the Add button according to each line where you entered a desired quantity

How to see all the Commands ?

Click on the Command's Button and then you can view a desired command and you can also print the command if you want

How to see the current invoice

Click on the Active Invoice Button and visualise the bill which is going on, then you can either cancel the bill or continue to add articles
